    ✦ I’m currently pursuing my B.S. in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras. My journey into data started with a natural curiosity to understand how things work — and that curiosity soon evolved into a passion for analyzing data, solving problems, and making sense of patterns hidden beneath the surface.

    ✦ I enjoy working with tools like SQL, Python, Tableau, and Excel to explore data and uncover insights. Whether it’s building interactive dashboards, optimizing queries, or automating reports, I find excitement in every part of the process that transforms raw data into actionable stories.
